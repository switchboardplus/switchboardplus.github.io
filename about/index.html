Industry-Leading Compliance

Switchboard Plus meets rigorous security standards like SOC II, GDPR, and CCPA. By continuously adopting new frameworks, we ensure your data remains protected and compliant.

Data Ownership & Privacy

Your data stays yours. We never use it outside your permissions, maintaining full alignment with privacy regulations and your specific requirements.

Secure Hosting

We host your data in sandboxed, single-tenant environments with dedicated VPCs, isolating your information for maximum protection and regulatory compliance.

Audit Logging

Gain visibility into data usage with configurable logs. Export them to your SIEM for monitoring and receive alerts that support consistent compliance.

Strict Permissions

We enforce immediate changes to access rights, letting only authorized individuals see the data they're permitted to view-no exceptions.

Robust Encryption

All data is encrypted at rest with AES 256 and in transit with TLS 1.2+. We deploy strong exfiltration controls to keep information secure.

We align with OWASP best practices to combat threats like injection and broken access control. For PCI DSS, we employ measures that protect payment card data and uphold confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

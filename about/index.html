About Switchboard+

BY THE NUMBERS

Operating since 2012
Active extensions 28K+
Average call latency 38ms

First-response time <90s

We've spent over a decade building communications infrastructure that businesses depend on every minute of every day – with a singular obsession for uptime, security, and the human on the other end of the line.

Founded in 2012, Switchboard+ began with a single belief: that small and mid-sized businesses shouldn't have to choose between enterprise-grade reliability and a bill they can actually understand.

Today we operate sandboxed, single-tenant infrastructure in carrier-neutral data centers across the U.S., serving healthcare practices, law firms, contact centers, and growing teams who'd rather not babysit a phone system.

We pick up our own phones. We answer our own tickets. And we put a thousand dollars on the line every time we sign you up.

We align with OWASP best practices to combat injection and broken access control. For PCI DSS, we employ controls that protect payment card data and uphold confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Switchboardplus, LLC ("Switchboard Plus," "we," "us," or "our") provides cloud-based Voice over IP (VoIP), call management, contact center, fax, voicemail, SMS/MMS messaging, and related communication services (collectively, the "Services").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, retain, and safeguard personal information when you visit our website, communicate with us, or use the Services. It also explains our practices for SMS and MMS text messaging, including how mobile opt-in information is handled.

This Privacy Policy applies to information that we collect directly from customers, prospective customers, website visitors, support contacts, and other individuals who interact with us. When we process information on behalf of a business customer through the Services, that customer may control the information and its own privacy notice may also apply.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact and Business Information: Name, business name, email address, mailing address, telephone number, and information provided when requesting a quote, contacting support, or communicating with us.
  • Account Information: Usernames, authentication information, account preferences, authorized-user details, and other information used to create and administer an account.
  • Billing Information: Billing address, transaction details, and payment-related information. Payment card information may be processed by our payment processors rather than stored directly by us.
  • Support and Communication Content: Information included in support tickets, emails, calls, forms, chats, and other communications with us.

Information Generated Through the Services

  • Call, Fax, Voicemail, and Messaging Data: Telephone numbers, call and message routing information, timestamps, duration, delivery status, message content, fax content, voicemail content, recordings, transcripts, and related metadata, depending on the features used and the customer's configuration.
  • SMS/MMS Consent and Preference Records: Mobile telephone numbers, the date and method of opt-in, consent records, opt-out requests, keyword responses, delivery records, and messaging preferences.
  • Usage and Technical Data: Features used, activity logs, diagnostic information, IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and connection information.

Information from Integrations and Other Sources

If a customer connects the Services to a third-party platform, such as a CRM or EMR system, we may receive information from that platform as authorized by the customer and subject to the applicable integration settings.

3. How We Use Information

We may use personal information to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and support the Services;
  • Create and administer accounts and process transactions;
  • Route calls, messages, faxes, and other communications;
  • Respond to inquiries, support requests, and service-related communications;
  • Authenticate users and protect the security and integrity of the Services;
  • Monitor performance, troubleshoot issues, and improve our products and Services;
  • Comply with applicable law, legal process, and contractual obligations;
  • Maintain records of consent, messaging preferences, and opt-out requests;
  • Send administrative notices and other communications related to the Services; and
  • Send marketing communications where permitted by law and consistent with your choices.

4. SMS/MMS Messaging Privacy and Opt-Out

Switchboard Plus may use SMS or MMS messaging to provide help desk responses, customer support, service notifications, account-related communications, and other communications requested or authorized by the recipient. Message frequency varies based on the nature of the interaction. Message and data rates may apply.

Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing any product or service. You may withdraw consent at any time by replying STOP, QUIT, END, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, or OPT OUT to a message. After an opt-out request is processed, you may receive one final message confirming that no further messages will be sent, unless you later provide consent again.

For assistance, reply HELP or contact us at connect@switchboard-plus.com or 845-444-8273.

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories of disclosure described in this Privacy Policy exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

5. How We Disclose Information

We do not sell personal information. All disclosure categories described below exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third party. Mobile telephone numbers will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Subject to these restrictions, we may disclose other personal information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: To vendors that perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, payment processing, analytics, security, customer support, and communications infrastructure. These providers may use information only as necessary to perform contracted services for us.
  • Legal and Safety Reasons: When reasonably necessary to comply with law, regulation, subpoena, court order, or other legal process; to enforce agreements; to protect rights, property, or safety; or to detect, prevent, or investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents.
  • Business Transactions: In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, sale of assets, or similar corporate transaction, subject to appropriate confidentiality and data-protection measures.
  • At Your Direction: When you instruct us to disclose other personal information or otherwise provide valid consent. This provision does not apply to text messaging originator opt-in data or SMS/MMS consent, which cannot be transferred or shared with another business, affiliate, or third party.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember preferences, understand site usage, and improve performance. Browser settings may allow you to block or delete cookies, although some site features may not function properly as a result.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Services, maintain business and consent records, comply with legal and contractual obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information, customer configuration, legal requirements, and the purpose for which the information was collected.

8. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. Depending on the applicable Service and configuration, these safeguards may include encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, logging, monitoring, and environment isolation.

No system or method of transmission is completely secure. We therefore cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to maintain safeguards appropriate to the nature of the information we process.

9. Privacy Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live and subject to applicable exceptions, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or a copy of certain personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing. You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To submit a privacy request, contact us using the information in Section 14. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request. You may also use an authorized agent where permitted by law.

You may opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in the email. To opt out of SMS/MMS messages, use the instructions in Section 4.

10. Children's Privacy

The Services are intended for businesses and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If we learn that such information has been collected, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

11. Third-Party Services and Integrations

The Services may contain links to, or integrate with, third-party websites and services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own notices. We encourage you to review those notices before providing information to a third party or enabling an integration.

12. International Data Transfers

Personal information may be processed in the United States and other countries where we or our service providers operate. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers of personal information.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised version will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Material changes may also be communicated through the Services or by other appropriate means.
